How to Use Frempo

1
Set your frame rate, frame range, and optionally load audio
2
Click TAP TEMPO to start the timeline and establish your beat
3
Continue tapping at regular intervals to lock in the tempo
4
Red markers will appear on the timeline showing where each beat lands
5
Click TAP TEMPO again to pause/stop the timeline
6
Click Reset to clear everything and start over
