How to Use Frempo

1
Set your frame rate and frame range below
2
Press SPACEBAR or Start to begin the 3-2-1 countdown
3
The blue playhead travels the timeline at your chosen FPS
4
Tap SPACEBAR or TAP BEAT to drop red beat markers
5
Drag the purple bar in the dark strip to zoom & pan the timeline
6
Click any red beat marker to remove it
